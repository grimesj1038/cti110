About
Hi, I'm Jasmine Grimes
Photographer based in North Carolina

Hi! I'm Jasmine and I'm a nature and portrait photographer in the cute town of Fayetteville. I live in a cosy 2 story with my family. My favourite things in the world are anime and crafting, and when I'm not shooting you'll find me on xbox. I couldn't live without tea, webnovels and good food. I am a very family oriented person and love spending time with them.

My love of the outdoors and people is what got me into photography. I've been a photographer for 3 years and I can't imagine doing anything else. My photography styles are traditional, artistic, and natural, and I just love capturing people's fascinating personalities. The best thing about being a photographer is that I get to meet wonderful new people and work for myself.

My clients choose me for my knack of making them look and feel fabulous. They want awesome photos of themselves and are excited to look and feel incredible. You're looking for a portrait photographer who will make you feel comfortable and who creates images that show your personality – and that's exactly what I do. A portrait session with me is laid back and informal, and it'll be just like hanging out with a friend.

Your shoot is all about having fun. Whether it's photos for your marketing materials or just for fun, I promise you'll enjoy yourself and leave feeling like you can take on the world. Shoots take place in your favourite spots around the city or in the studio and sessions last for 2 to 4 hours. If this sounds like you, then get in touch to book your session! Contact Me
